Employee Workforce Analytics Dashboard

An interactive **Excel Dashboard** designed to analyze and visualize key human resources (HR) and workforce metrics. This project provides valuable insights into employee demographics, departmental distributions, payroll, overtime hours, and regional presence, empowering organizations to make data-driven HR decisions.

---

## 📊 Dashboard Overview

The **Employee Workforce Analytics** dashboard offers a high-level executive summary of workforce distribution and cost analysis, featuring interactive slicers for deep-dive exploration.

### Key Metrics (KPIs)
*   **Total Employees:** 237,705
*   **Total Payroll:** $17,099,892
*   **Average Salary:** $24,818.42
*   **Average Job Rate:** 3.59 / 5.00
*   **Total Overtime Hours:** 9,441 Hours

---

## 🔍 Key Insights & Visualizations

The dashboard contains four core analytical views:

1.  **Top 10 Departments by Employee Count (Column Chart):** Shows the distribution of staff across major departments. *Manufacturing* is the largest department (140 employees), followed by *Quality Control* (89) and *Account Management* (84).
2.  **Top 10 Departments by Overtime Hours (Bar Chart):** Highlights where overtime is most heavily utilized. *Quality Control* and *Manufacturing* lead with approximately 1,700 hours each, pointing to areas with high operational demand.
3.  **Top 10 Departments by Average Salary (Bar Chart):** Details the compensation levels across different business units, assisting in payroll benchmarking.
4.  **Employees by Country (Pie Chart):** Illustrates the geographic distribution of the workforce:
    *   **Egypt:** 55%
    *   **Lebanon:** 23%
    *   **Saudi Arabia:** 13%
    *   **Syria:** 8%
    *   *Other:* 1%

## ⚙️ Interactive Features (Slicers)

Users can dynamically filter the entire dashboard using the following interactive slicers:
*   **Gender:** Filter metrics by Female or Male employees.
*   **Center:** Analyze performance across regional hubs (East, Main, North, South, West).
*   **Department:** Focus on specific functional areas (e.g., Creative, Account Management).
*   **Country:** Drill down into specific country-level data (Egypt, Lebanon, Saudi Arabia, Syria, etc.).

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies Used
*   **Microsoft Excel:** Core tool for data modeling, analysis, and visualization.
*   **Pivot Tables & Pivot Charts:** Used to aggregate and summarize raw workforce data.
*   **Advanced Formulas:** To calculate key performance indicators (KPIs).
*   **Interactive Slicers:** Connected to multiple pivot tables to enable synchronized interactive filtering.
